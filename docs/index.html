SwedeHF database 4 (SHFDB4)

A Heart Failure cohort from Sweden 2000 - 2021. The population consists of 1) patients registered in SwedeHF, 2) recorded in the National Patient Register with a ICD-10 code in main position for HF and 3) gender, year of birth and county of residence matched HF-free controls.
